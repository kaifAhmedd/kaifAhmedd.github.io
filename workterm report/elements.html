About Entact Solutions Inc.

Entact Solutions Inc. is a tech-integrated trucking company that uses innovative software solutions to support and assit truck driver and fleet managers. Thier application, Entact HOS, is a user-friendly application deisgned to improve the safety, compliance, and efficiency on the road. By combining UI/UX design with mobile accessibility, real-time alerts, and detailed performance reports, Entact Solutions helps to ensure that drivers stay safe and compliant with the Federal Carrier Safety Administration regulations while enhancing their overall trucking experience.
